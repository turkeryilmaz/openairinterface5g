 SionnaRT CIR Dataset Metadata

 - bandwidth_pass       : Passband bandwidth in Hz
 - l_min                : Smallest time lag represented (in samples or seconds)
 - l_max                : Largest time lag (number of taps)
 - sampling_frequency   : CIR sampling rate in Hz (default: Nyquist, 1/bandwidth)
 - normalize            : If true, CIRs are energy-normalized across all snapshots
 - normalize_delays     : If true, time delays are normalized to align with system
